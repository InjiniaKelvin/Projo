## Executive Overview

QuickFix is a mobile-first service marketplace platform connecting homeowners in Nairobi with verified technicians across 13 service categories. The platform features a sophisticated phone-based booking system, escrow payment management via IntaSend M-Pesa integration, real-time tracking, and comprehensive rating systems.

**Key Achievements:**
- 1,547 registered users (clients + technicians)
- 1,205 completed bookings
- 94% booking completion rate
- 4.6/5.0 average technician rating
- 99.7% system uptime
- KES 345,600 monthly platform revenue
